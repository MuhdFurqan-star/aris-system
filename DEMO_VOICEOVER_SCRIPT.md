# ARIS — Demo Voiceover Script
### Academic Replacement Intelligence System
**Total time: ~8 minutes · 3 roles: Admin · Lecturer · Student**

> **How to use this:** Read the *VOICEOVER* lines out loud while you record your screen.
> Do the *[SCREEN]* actions at the same time. Speak slow and clear. Easy words only.
> Target speaking speed is normal talking — about 130 words a minute.
> The lines marked **(quick list)** are fast — just point at the menu while you say them.

---

## INTRO — (0:00 – 0:30)

**[SCREEN]** Show the ARIS login page. Move the mouse over the logo.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Hi everyone. This is ARIS — the Academic Replacement Intelligence System.
It is a website that helps a college manage class replacements and attendance.
There are three kinds of users: the Admin, the Lecturer, and the Student.
It also sends notifications when things change, and it even works on the phone, offline.
Let me show you what each one can do. Let's start."

---

# ROLE 1 — ADMIN  (0:30 – 2:45)

**[SCREEN]** Log in as Admin. Land on the Admin dashboard (show the charts/numbers).

**VOICEOVER:**
"First is the Admin — the boss of the system.
The dashboard shows me numbers and charts: how many lecturers, how many students, and how many requests are waiting."

**[SCREEN]** Open **Manage Users**. Show add user / reset password.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Here the Admin adds lecturers and students, edits them, and can reset a password if someone forgets."

**[SCREEN]** Open **Subjects** and **Semesters**. Show a subject given to a lecturer, and the **Enrollment** page where students are assigned to a class.

**VOICEOVER:**
"The Admin also sets up the subjects and the class groups.
Every subject is given to one lecturer. And in Enrollment, the Admin puts each student into their class.
This tells the system who teaches what, and who studies where."

**[SCREEN]** Open **Venues**. Show add a room, then **block** a room for a date range.

**VOICEOVER:**
"The Admin adds the venues — the rooms. They can even block a room for some dates, like when it's under repair, so no class gets put there."

**[SCREEN]** Open **Replacement Requests**. Open a pending one, click **Approve**.

**VOICEOVER:**
"When a lecturer asks to move a class, it comes here.
The Admin sees the reason, the old time, and the new time. If it's good, they click Approve.
The lecturer and the students get a notification and an email right away."

**[SCREEN]** *(quick list)* Hover the side menu over Feedback, Reports, and Heatmap.

**VOICEOVER (quick list):**
"The Admin can also do a few more things: read the room feedback and mark it resolved, make replacement and feedback reports and download them as CSV or PDF, and open a venue heatmap to see which rooms are used the most.
That's the Admin. Now the Lecturer."

---

# ROLE 2 — LECTURER  (2:45 – 5:30)

**[SCREEN]** Log in as a Lecturer. Show the Lecturer dashboard.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Next is the Lecturer — the teacher. This is their dashboard."

**[SCREEN]** Click **My Classes**. Show the weekly timetable of *own* subjects only.

**VOICEOVER:**
"This is My Classes. It shows only the subjects this lecturer teaches, in a weekly timetable. Clean and easy."

**[SCREEN]** Click a class cell → popup opens. Click **Replace Class** → form opens.

**VOICEOVER:**
"When I click a class, a popup opens with two main buttons.
The first is Replace Class — for when the teacher can't make it.
They pick the old time slot from a dropdown, or type it by hand. Then they choose the new date and time."

**[SCREEN]** After picking new date + time, show the AI listing available venues ranked.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Now the smart part — the AI.
Once they pick the new date and time, the system finds every room that is free at that moment, and ranks the best ones on top.
The lecturer picks one, adds a reason, and sends it to the Admin. Done."

**[SCREEN]** Back to My Classes → click a class → **Generate QR**. Show the QR page + 15-min timer.

**VOICEOVER:**
"The second button is Generate QR, for attendance.
The system makes a QR code for that class, that day, and that time. It only works for fifteen minutes, so no one can scan it later.
The lecturer shows it on screen, and students scan it."

**[SCREEN]** Open **Attendance Report** → per-subject table → open one subject → one session.

**VOICEOVER:**
"After class, the Attendance Report shows every subject and how many students came.
I can open a subject, then a class, and see exactly who was present and who was absent. Fast and fair."

**[SCREEN]** *(quick list)* Hover over My Requests, Past Replacements, and Venue Feedback.

**VOICEOVER (quick list):**
"The lecturer can also check My Requests to see if it's approved, look at Past Replacements for history, and leave Venue Feedback to rate a room after using it.
Now let's see the Student."

---

# ROLE 3 — STUDENT  (5:30 – 7:30)

**[SCREEN]** Log in as a Student. Show the student dashboard.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Last is the Student. This dashboard is simple and made for the phone too."

**[SCREEN]** Click **Weekly Timetable**. Show their own schedule (blue + gold cards).

**VOICEOVER:**
"Here the student sees their full week — all their classes in one place.
Normal classes are blue, replacement classes are gold, so they never get confused. A moved class shows the new room and time clearly."

**[SCREEN]** Click **Scan Attendance QR**. Allow camera. Point at the lecturer's QR.

**VOICEOVER:**
"This is the best part for students — Scan Attendance QR.
They open the camera right inside the app and point it at the lecturer's code.
One scan, and attendance is marked. No paper, no sign-in sheet."

**[SCREEN]** Show the success / confirmation screen.

**VOICEOVER:**
"The system checks the code is still valid and that the student really belongs to that class.
If it's okay, it says success. Each student scans only once, so no one can mark for a friend."

**[SCREEN]** Open the **Chatbot**. Type "Stats" or "Help" to show the chips.

**VOICEOVER:**
"There's also a chat helper. The student can ask quick things — like their stats, or get help — and the bot answers, like a little assistant."

**[SCREEN]** *(quick list)* Show **Search replacements** + **Bookmark** a class, then the **Export (.ics)** button.

**VOICEOVER (quick list):**
"Students can also search all replacement classes and bookmark the ones they care about.
And with one click, they export their bookmarks and timetable to Google or Apple Calendar.
That's everything for the student."

---

## OUTRO — (7:30 – 8:00)

**[SCREEN]** Show the notification bell ringing, then flip quickly through the three dashboards.

**VOICEOVER:**
"And across the whole app, the notification bell keeps everyone updated, and it all works on the phone, even offline.
So that's ARIS.
The Admin runs the system. The Lecturer plans classes, uses AI to find free rooms, and takes attendance with QR. And the Student checks their timetable and scans to mark attendance.
Everything works together to make class replacements and attendance easy and smart.
Thanks for watching."

### ⏱️ Quick Timing Cheat Sheet
| Section | Time | Key thing to show |
|---|---|---|
| Intro | 0:00–0:30 | Login page, three roles, notifications + phone |
| Admin | 0:30–2:45 | Users, subjects, enrollment, venues + block, approve, then quick list (feedback, reports CSV/PDF, heatmap) |
| Lecturer | 2:45–5:30 | My Classes, AI venue suggest, QR generate, attendance report, then quick list (my requests, past, venue feedback) |
| Student | 5:30–7:30 | Timetable, scan QR, chatbot, then quick list (search, bookmark, export) |
| Outro | 7:30–8:00 | Notification bell + wrap up all three roles |

### ✅ Feature Coverage (every page in the app)
- **Admin:** dashboard + charts, manage users + reset password, subjects, semesters, **enrollment**, venues + **blocking**, approve/reject requests, **feedback + resolve**, **reports (CSV + PDF)**, **venue heatmap**.
- **Lecturer:** dashboard, My Classes timetable, **AI venue suggest**, create request, **QR generate**, **attendance report + per-session detail**, my requests, past replacements, **venue feedback**, full class timetable.
- **Student:** dashboard, weekly timetable, **scan QR**, **search replacements**, **bookmark**, **chatbot**, **calendar export (.ics)**.
- **Shared:** **notifications**, **PWA / offline**.

### 🎬 Recording Tips
- Log in to all three accounts **before** recording so switching is fast.
- Have one **pending** replacement request ready for the Admin to approve.
- Have the **QR page open on one screen** and the **student scanner / phone** ready so the scan works live.
- For the **(quick list)** parts, just hover the menu — don't open every page — to save time.
- Speak a little slower than normal; it sounds clearer on video.
- **If you go over 8 minutes, cut in this order:** (1) Admin reports/heatmap quick list, (2) chatbot, (3) Admin venue-blocking line.
